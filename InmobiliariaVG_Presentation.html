Executive Summary & Justification

Why this platform exists

The "Silver Economy" — affluent retirees seeking a safe, affordable, high-quality life abroad — is a large and growing market. But the buying journey is broken: it is fragmented across agents, lawyers, banks and movers, conducted in a foreign language, and riddled with bureaucracy that frightens cautious older buyers. InmobiliariaVG unifies the entire journey and removes that fear.

😰

The problem

A retiree abroad faces hidden costs, legal jargon, currency confusion, visa complexity and the risk of isolation — across a dozen disconnected providers.

🧭

The solution

One platform that guides the buyer through all 12 stages, pairs them with vetted human specialists, and translates every step into plain English.

💚

The promise

"Peace of mind" delivered end to end: trust, security and simplicity over complexity — turning a transaction into a new life.

A continuous zoom from the home page, into a navigable map, into an immersive property ficha — then a guided path through negotiation, legal & migration, and finally arrival and belonging.

⭐ The conversion gate

The single most important crossing: the buyer's explicit, opt-in decision to "quote this home & be contacted." It marks the shift from self-service to an accompanied consultancy — and the quote travels into the CRM so the advisor opens already knowing everything. Warm, opt-in conversion over aggressive lead capture.

Delivery

Implementation roadmap

MVP-first and de-risked: validate demand with a lean immersive marketplace, then layer the consultancy, the CRM, and the deeper integrations as they mature — some paced to the open-finance regulatory rollout.

Phase 1MVP · 8–12 wks

Immersive marketplace MVP

Home, navigable map with first data layers, property ficha with embedded 360° tours + info hotspots, lifestyle search, save & compare, transparent quote simulator with dual currency. Accessibility baseline. Validates demand fast, low risk.
