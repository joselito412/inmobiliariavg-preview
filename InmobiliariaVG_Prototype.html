Interactive Prototype Walkthrough

The full InmobiliariaVG experience, screen by screen

Every step of the 12-stage journey — buyer-facing UX/UI and the internal sales CRM — shown as the actual interface, with the functionality of each screen explained beside it.

How to read this

One continuous experience

The buyer moves through a continuous zoom — home → map → ficha → immersion — then into an accompanied path of negotiation, legal & migration support, and finally arrival and community. Each screen below shows the interface and what it does.

Navigation

Continuous zoom — four levels into the experience

The interface behaves like a map zooming in: home → map → ficha → fullscreen. A single back gesture zooms out. The buyer never feels they're loading a new page.

Behavior

Zoom in magnifies a card/pin into the next full view
Zoom out returns you exactly where you left off
Mobile-first: full-screen map + draggable card sheet
Reduced-motion fallback replaces zoom with a cross-fade
Phase I

Discovery, selection & immersion

Autonomous and asynchronous — the buyer explores alone, at their own pace, with no pressure and no forced sign-up.

STAGE 1 · LEVEL 1

Home / e-commerce

Two paths only: talk to the chatbot, or tap a huge property-type card. Zero overwhelm.

What this screen does

Chatbot-first: describe a feeling in plain English
Huge icon cards for apto, casa, finca, lote, mansión, penthouse…
Tapping any card zooms into the map, pre-filtered
“Talk to a Human” pinned on every screen
STAGE 1 · LEVEL 2

Navigable map with data layers

Airbnb-style map plus selectable intelligence layers: safety heatmap, service pins, per-property scores.

What this screen does

3 layer types: zone heatmaps, service points, per-property scores
Plain-language labels not GIS jargon
Heatmaps exclusive (one at a time); services combine freely
Presets flip several layers at once; every layer cites source + date
Pins & cards in sync; data mostly from external APIs / open data
STAGE 3 · LEVEL 3

Property ficha (smart container)

The layout never changes; the viewer auto-selects the most immersive mode available. Key data always visible.

What this screen does

Adaptive viewer: 3D if a model exists, else 360/video, else photos
Empty modes auto-hide — never a dead button
Always-on data panel: price, cost of living, safety, clinic, ROI
Tooltips translate local terms (escritura, estrato…)
Two ways into immersion: expand button or “walk through”
STAGE 3b

Enriched ficha — map layers, landed

The map's layers become this home's scores. Same data system, two scales — building consistency and trust.

What this screen does

“Your lifestyle match” is personalized by the buyer's filters
Contributing scores shown as labeled bars
Nearby services inherit the exact map-layer data
Each score is tappable → plain-English formula + source
Consistency between map & ficha is what builds deep trust
STAGE 3 · LEVEL 4

Fullscreen immersive mode

The viewer expands edge-to-edge; the UI gets out of the way. One dock: navigate 3D, zoom images, play video.

What this screen does

Edge-to-edge immersion; only floating translucent controls
One dock: navigate 3D · zoom images · play video
“Jump to room” dollhouse for users who won't drag
Accessibility & “Get Help” never leave the screen
Dark stage on purpose (cinematic) with one-tap exit
Phase II

Negotiation — quote, decide, contact, sign

Self-service crosses into an accompanied consultancy at one explicit, opt-in decision. From here the buyer is never alone.

STAGE 5

Interactive payment simulator

Built for a foreign retiree: cash / builder / mortgage, dual currency, real-time FX, honest total-to-close.

What this screen does

3 payment paths (cash / builder / mortgage), combinable
Dual currency on every figure + real-time FX rate
Surfaces the hidden currency-conversion spread
Construction-milestone schedule for pre-sale
Total interest disclosed; soft exits + handoff to specialist
STAGE 5b

Lenders & live mortgage rates

Financial entities with current rates, conditions, source & freshness — justified by Colombia's mandatory open finance.

What this screen does

Layered sourcing: partner feeds + regulator data + curated fallback
Freshness badges: LIVE / OFFICIAL DATA / CURATED
Conditions shown (term, down %, foreigner eligibility), not just the rate
Justified by Decree 0368/2026 — mandatory open finance
Picking a lender feeds its rate into the payment calc
CONVERSION GATE

Self-service becomes accompanied

The single most important crossing: the buyer's explicit, opt-in decision to be contacted. No pressure, no obligation.

What this screen does

Opt-in, not opt-out — contact only happens if the buyer says yes
Two honest paths, equal respect; “not yet” is never hidden or shamed
“No obligation” stated plainly — contact ≠ pressure to buy
The quote becomes the lead; terms travel into the CRM
This crossing is the primary conversion metric
STAGE 4

Contact & negotiate with a sales specialist

A named human takes over. One hub: live video tour or chat, in the buyer's timezone, with the quote already in hand.

What this screen does

A named, real human with face, languages & reviews takes over
Buyer's choice of channel: video for the brave, chat for the cautious
Timezone-aware scheduling
The quote terms travel in — no friction, no repeating
Mirrored by the agent CRM (see seller side)
STAGE 6

Promise signature via Document Factory

A minimal wizard collects contract data, sends it to the separate Document Factory, then returns it for review & secure signing.

What this screen does

InmobiliariaVG doesn't generate docs — a separate Document Factory does
Minimal step-by-step wizard collects contract data (pre-filled)
Honest waiting state, then review (plain-English + PDF + voice)
Verified-ID e-signature via the factory's signing service
Integration contract: POST /documents · webhook · signature endpoint
Phase III

Legal, financial & migration concierge

The consultancy core. One calm dashboard replaces bureaucratic chaos, with a named specialist per track.

STAGES 7–9

Concierge dashboard

Three specialist tracks run in parallel, color-coded by status, with a named expert each. The shared memory of the team-by-specialty model.

What this screen does

Finite list + visible progress turns bureaucracy into clarity
A named specialist per track — always know who to ask
Color-coded status: done / in progress / up next
Shared file: nobody re-explains their situation
Each track is a guided, conversational workspace
Phase IV

Closing, relocation & integration

Deliver on the promise and build belonging — keys, moving, home adaptation, and long-term community.

STAGES 10–11

Closing, relocation & arrival

Logistics and a warm landing: keys, shipping, utilities, and senior-friendly home adaptation before arrival.

What this screen does

Closing timeline: deed, key handover, shipping, utilities
International shipping tracked door-to-door, customs handled
Senior-friendly home adaptation before arrival
Healthcare onboarding + insurance setup
A furnished, connected, safe home on day one
STAGE 12

Long-term support & community

The journey doesn't end at the sale — it becomes the buyer's ongoing home: concierge, property management, belonging.

What this screen does

Ongoing concierge: a single point of contact for anything
Property management = recurring revenue (tenants, income reports)
Community events fight isolation — the deepest relocation fear
A happy, connected retiree drives referrals
Belonging is the real product; the sale was just the start
Seller side

The sales-agent CRM

Minimalist and traditional by design — dense, fast, keyboard-friendly. Shares one data system with the buyer platform.

CRM

Pipeline (agent home screen)

A kanban mirroring the buyer journey. Leads arrive with full context; a green border marks journey-sourced leads.

What this screen does

Kanban mirrors the buyer journey stages
Green border = journey-sourced lead with context attached
Context badges: booked tour, favorites, quote locked
Drag-and-drop between stages
Dense, fast, keyboard-friendly back-office UX
CRM

Lead detail — context + communication

Two panes of equal weight: the buyer's auto-attached context, and integrated chat / Meet / Gmail. One shared timeline.

What this screen does

Context pane: property, locked quote, plan, lifestyle match — auto-attached
Comms pane: chat (bridged to WhatsApp), Meet, Gmail, scheduling
Quick actions: video tour, schedule, send PDF, hand off to legal
Scheduled events appear inline in the thread
Everything writes back to one shared timeline
CRM

Inventory & reports

Manage listings (with a 3D indicator — a conversion driver) and track performance: conversion, pipeline value, response time.

What this screen does

Inventory table with a 3D-media indicator (listings with 3D convert best)
Publishing here surfaces a property in the buyer map & ficha
KPIs: new leads, conversion, pipeline value, response time
Funnel visualization; managers see it aggregated across agents
Roles & permissions govern who sees what
End to end

One promise, delivered across 12 stages

From an intimidating cross-border purchase to a calm, guided path to a peaceful new life — buyer-facing warmth and seller-side efficiency, sharing one data system. Full detail in the UX/UI and CRM specifications in this folder.
